<p><p>The SiC grinding and polishing equipment market is divided into two main types: coarse grinding equipment and fine grinding equipment. Coarse grinding equipment is designed for heavy material removal, making it suitable for initial machining processes to shape silicon carbide materials efficiently. Fine grinding equipment, on the other hand, focuses on achieving a smooth surface finish, enabling precision polishing applications that enhance material quality. Together, these equipment types play a crucial role in the production and refinement of silicon carbide products.</p></p>
