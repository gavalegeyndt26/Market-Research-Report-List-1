<p><p>The polycarbonate sheets and films market comprises two main types: Polycarbonate Film, which has a thickness of 0.25 mm or less, and Polycarbonate Sheet, which exceeds this thickness. Polycarbonate Films are typically used for applications requiring flexibility, lightweight properties, and ease of installation, such as in packaging and surface protection. In contrast, Polycarbonate Sheets are favored for their durability, impact resistance, and UV protection, making them ideal for structural applications in construction, automotive, and electrical industries.</p></p>
