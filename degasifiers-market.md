<p><p>Degasifiers are essential systems used to remove dissolved gases, particularly in water treatment and industrial processes. The market is primarily divided into two types: packed towers and tray towers. Packed towers utilize structured packing materials to enhance gas-liquid contact, improving efficiency and deployment in various applications. In contrast, tray towers rely on trays to create stages for gas and liquid phases to interact. Both types offer distinct advantages in efficiency, design flexibility, and suitability for different operational conditions.</p></p>
