<p><p>The proton therapy equipment market includes various types of particle accelerators. Synchrotrons generate high-energy protons by utilizing magnetic fields to accelerate particles in a circular path. Cyclotrons, on the other hand, produce protons using a spiral path and are typically more compact. Synchrocyclotrons adapt the design of cyclotrons with varying frequencies to maintain efficiency at lower energies. Linear accelerators (linacs) deliver protons in a straight line, often used for specific applications, offering versatility in treating different cancer types.</p></p>
