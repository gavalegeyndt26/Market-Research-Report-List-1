<p><p>The Advanced Suspension Control System market features two primary types: semi-active and active suspension systems. Semi-active suspension systems adjust damping characteristics based on road conditions, enhancing ride comfort and stability without a full energy input. In contrast, active suspension systems actively manage ride height and stiffness using sensors and actuators, offering superior vehicle dynamics and handling. Both systems aim to improve driving experiences, enhance safety, and optimize vehicle performance across various terrains and driving conditions.</p></p>
