<p><p>The hot foil stamping market features three primary machine types based on their stamping surfaces. Flat-flat foil stamping machines are ideal for flat materials, offering precise and consistent impressions. Round-round machines are designed for cylindrical objects, ensuring even foil application on curved surfaces. Round-flat foil stamping machines combine both flat and round capabilities, accommodating diverse shapes and substrates, making them versatile for various applications. Each type serves specific needs within packaging, labeling, and decorative printing industries.</p></p>
