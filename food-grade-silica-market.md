<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Fumed Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food-grade silica market comprises primarily two types: precipitated silica and fumed silica. Precipitated silica is produced through a chemical process, offering high purity and absorption properties, making it ideal for applications like anti-caking agents in powdered foods. Fumed silica, created by combusting silicon tetrachloride in oxygen, possesses a highly porous structure and is commonly used for its thickening and stabilizing abilities in various food products. Both types enhance food quality and stability, ensuring safety and consumer satisfaction.</p></p>
