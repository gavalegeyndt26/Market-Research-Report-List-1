<p><strong>The Chip Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dielectric Chip Antennas</li><li>LTCC Chip Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chip antenna market is primarily divided into two types: dielectric chip antennas and LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) chip antennas. Dielectric chip antennas utilize high-permittivity materials to achieve compact designs with enhanced performance, often suited for consumer electronics and IoT applications. In contrast, LTCC chip antennas are fabricated using a multilayer process that allows integration of passive components, resulting in superior performance, thermal stability, and robustness, making them ideal for automotive and telecommunications sectors.</p></p>
