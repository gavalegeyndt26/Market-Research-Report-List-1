<p><p>The electroosmosis generator market features two primary types: compound pulse and high voltage pulse systems. Compound pulse electroosmosis generators utilize a series of combined voltage pulses, enhancing the movement of fluids through porous materials, which improves efficiency in applications such as soil remediation and wastewater treatment. High voltage pulse generators, on the other hand, emit short, intense voltage spikes that promote rapid fluid movement, making them ideal for processes requiring quick response times and higher energy efficiency. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
