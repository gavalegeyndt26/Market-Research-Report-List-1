<p><strong>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flow Cell Based System</li><li>Direct Read System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer market primarily features two types: flow cell-based systems and direct read systems. Flow cell-based systems utilize a continuous stream of samples and reagents, allowing for precise measurements and rapid analysis. In contrast, direct read systems analyze samples in discrete wells, providing immediate results without the need for sample mixing. Both systems enhance laboratory efficiency and accuracy, catering to different analytical needs in various sectors, including environmental testing, clinical diagnostics, and industrial applications.</p></p>
