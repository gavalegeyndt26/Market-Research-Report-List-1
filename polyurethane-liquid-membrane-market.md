<p><strong>The Polyurethane Liquid Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane</li><li>Two-component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane liquid membrane market is divided into two primary types: one-component and two-component polyurethane liquid membranes. One-component membranes consist of a ready-to-use formulation that cures upon exposure to moisture, offering convenience and ease of application. In contrast, two-component membranes require a separate curing agent to be mixed with the base material prior to application, resulting in superior durability and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used for waterproofing and protecting surfaces in various construction applications.</p></p>
