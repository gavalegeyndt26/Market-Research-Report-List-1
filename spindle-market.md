<p><ul><li>Electric Spindle</li><li>Mechanical Spindle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spindle market is categorized into electric and mechanical spindles. Electric spindles are powered by electric motors, offering higher precision and speed control, making them ideal for CNC machining applications. They are often used in industries requiring fine machining, such as aerospace and automotive. Mechanical spindles operate through traditional mechanical means, leveraging belts or gears for power transmission. While they are generally more robust and cost-effective, they may provide less precise speed control compared to their electric counterparts.</p></p>
