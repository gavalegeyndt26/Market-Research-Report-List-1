<p><p>The large-scale HVAC system market comprises two main types: decentralized and centralized HVAC systems. Decentralized systems utilize multiple smaller units located throughout a building, offering flexibility and individual control over temperature and air quality. In contrast, centralized HVAC systems feature a single, large unit that serves the entire building, providing uniform climate control and potentially better energy efficiency. Each system serves distinct needs, allowing users to choose based on building size, layout, and specific climate control requirements.</p></p>
