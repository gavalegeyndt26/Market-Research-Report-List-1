<p><p>The chopped fiberglass mat market consists of various types, including C-Glass, HR-Glass, and SiO2-Glass. C-Glass is the most commonly used type, known for its cost-effectiveness and good mechanical properties, making it suitable for various applications. HR-Glass, or high-performance glass, offers superior strength and thermal resistance, ideal for demanding environments. SiO2-Glass, containing high silica content, provides excellent chemical resistance and is often used in specialized applications requiring enhanced durability. Each type serves distinct needs across industries like construction, automotive, and marine.</p></p>
