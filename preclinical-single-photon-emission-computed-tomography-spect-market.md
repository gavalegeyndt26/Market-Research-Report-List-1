<p><p>The preclinical Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) market is divided into two main types: structural imaging and functional imaging. Structural imaging focuses on visualizing the anatomy and morphology of biological tissues at a detailed level, aiding in assessments of disease progression. Functional imaging, on the other hand, measures physiological processes, such as blood flow and metabolic activity, providing insights into the functional status of tissues and organs. Together, these imaging modalities enhance understanding of complex biological systems in research settings.</p></p>
