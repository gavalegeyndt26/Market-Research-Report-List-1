<p><strong>The Silicon Bronze Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Silicon Bronze</li><li>High Silicon Bronze</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon bronze wire market is divided into two primary types: low silicon bronze and high silicon bronze. Low silicon bronze contains a lower percentage of silicon, providing good corrosion resistance and excellent weldability, making it suitable for marine and architectural applications. In contrast, high silicon bronze features a higher silicon content, enhancing strength, wear resistance, and thermal properties, making it ideal for industrial applications such as manufacturing and electrical components. Each type caters to specific requirements based on performance needs.</p></p>
