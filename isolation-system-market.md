<p><p>Isolation systems encompass various market types including shock absorbers, dampers, and vibration isolators. Shock absorbers primarily reduce the impact of sudden forces, enhancing vehicle and equipment stability. Dampers, on the other hand, control oscillations and energy dissipation, improving performance in structures and machinery. Vibration isolators minimize the transmission of vibrations from sources to sensitive equipment, essential in industrial and medical applications. Together, these systems enhance safety, comfort, and longevity across multiple industries by mitigating unwanted energy and motion.</p></p>
