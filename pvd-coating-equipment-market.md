<p><p>The PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating equipment market comprises various types that cater to different coating needs. Evaporation coating machines utilize thermal energy to vaporize materials, forming a thin film on substrates. Sputtering film coaters use a plasma discharge to eject atoms from a target material, enabling uniform coatings on complex geometries. Additionally, other market types may include hybrid systems or specialized equipment designed for unique applications, broadening the scope of PVD technology and increasing its adoption across diverse industries.</p></p>
