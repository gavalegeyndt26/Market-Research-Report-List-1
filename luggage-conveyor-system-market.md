<p><p>The luggage conveyor system market features various types, including delivery, injection, and queue conveyors. Delivery conveyors efficiently transport luggage from check-in areas to loading zones, enhancing airport operational flow. Injection conveyors facilitate the seamless transfer of bags from one system to another, improving overall efficiency. Queue conveyors manage passenger luggage waiting lines, optimizing baggage handling during peak times. Together, these systems streamline airport logistics, reduce handling times, and improve the overall travel experience for passengers.</p></p>
