<p><strong>The Bone Densitometer Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometry</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone densitometer systems are categorized into axial and peripheral bone densitometry types. Axial bone densitometry focuses on measuring bone mineral density (BMD) in central areas, primarily using dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA) for precise assessments of the spine and hip. Peripheral bone densitometry, on the other hand, targets smaller and more accessible areas, such as the wrist or heel, utilizing devices that are often portable and cost-effective, making them suitable for routine screenings and point-of-care testing.</p></p>
