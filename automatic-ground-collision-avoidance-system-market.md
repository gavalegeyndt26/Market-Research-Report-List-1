<p><p>The Automatic Ground Collision Avoidance System (AGCAS) market comprises two main types: ACAS (Automatic Collision Avoidance System) and ASAS (Advisory Collision Avoidance System). ACAS focuses on automatically executing maneuvers to prevent ground collisions, providing a high level of safety assurance for pilots during critical phases of flight. In contrast, ASAS serves as an advisory system, offering suggestions and alerts to pilots, enabling them to make informed decisions to avoid potential collisions while retaining manual control.</p></p>
