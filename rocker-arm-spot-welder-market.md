<p><p>The rocker arm spot welder market can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic welders. Manual welders require operator intervention for each weld, offering flexibility and control, making them ideal for small-scale applications. Semi-automatic welders streamline the process by automating certain functions, enhancing productivity while still requiring some operator input. Automatic welders operate independently, enabling high-volume production with minimal human intervention, making them suitable for large manufacturing environments where efficiency and consistency are crucial.</p></p>
