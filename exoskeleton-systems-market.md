<p><p>Exoskeleton systems are classified into three main types: lower body exoskeletons, full body exoskeletons, and upper body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons assist the legs with walking and lifting, enhancing mobility for rehabilitation or industrial use. Full body exoskeletons provide support to the entire physique, enabling a range of movements and activities, often for heavy lifting or mobility support. Upper body exoskeletons focus on the arms and torso, aiding in repetitive tasks, reducing fatigue, and improving ergonomics for manual labor.</p></p>
