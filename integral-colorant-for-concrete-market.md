<p><p>The integral colorant for concrete market is divided into two primary types: powder and liquid. Powder colorants consist of dry pigments mixed into the concrete before pouring, offering a varied range of hues and excellent UV stability. Liquid colorants, on the other hand, are concentrated pigments suspended in a liquid solution, providing ease of mixing and uniform distribution. Both types are designed to enhance the aesthetic appeal of concrete while maintaining durability and longevity in various applications.</p></p>
